Food Environment Atlas

United States

In each state there are different organizations that seek grants towards fresh food funding. Organizations in different states recieve funding based on their programs description. Depending on the program funding can be used for different purposes such as food campaings, farming projects, fresh food delivery, and other agricultural projects. Statistics show that more than 40 million American's rely on food funding programs throughout their daily lives. Access to healthy food is also another issue in America's society for individuals that participate in food funding programs. Since 2017 new estimates have indicated that the amount of hunger could increase amongst the American population. As a result many individual will loose access to healthy foods and funding programs.

Funding is also provided on various price ranges depending on the scope of the project.
