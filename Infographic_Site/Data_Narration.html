Data Narration

Overview

The food Environment Atlas Infographic focuses on areas within America's States. Each area indicates the access to food funding based on awarded grants. Food prices and access can have either a negative or positive impact on certain locations. The data displays different sources of food such as local stores, farms, farmers market and other food programs. The area plays an important role to help determine how much food is produced in one location by farmers based on funding. Color coding for each stay was used as a method in order to determine the states funding amount by chosen programs. Funding for food in america is a very important topic in present time. The colors red, orange and green symbolize the impact of funding on areas in america.

Behind The Image

The first image on the infographic page presents the idea of healthy food. Healthy food plays an important role in our society. It was important to make the colors in the illustration bold to bring attention to the subject matter. Placing the image first was used as a tool to give a better understanding of a subject matter. Acknowledging the idea that funding plays an important role for healthy food is the goal for this infographic.

United States

The United States is used as the entire focus for food environmental atlas. America as a whole depends on food funding progrmas that impacts many communities with negative and positive outcomes. More than 40 million americans depend on the outcome of food funding programs in their daily lives.
